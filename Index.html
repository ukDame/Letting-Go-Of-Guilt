Morning Reflection

What exactly am I feeling guilty about? (Action, thought, decision?)
Can I allow myself to feel guilt without judging myself for having it?
Can I say, "I made a mistake, but I am not a bad person."

Daily Practice

Am I guilty because I truly harmed someone, or because I fell short of my own impossible standards?
What part was within my control, and what wasn't?
Is there an apology, action, or change I can offer to repair the situation?

Evening Reflection

Can I choose to release the guilt for my own healing, regardless of how others respond?
What can this experience teach me about myself, my values, or my growth?
What would I say to a beloved friend in my shoes? Now, say it to myself.
What is one small step I can take today to live lighter and freer?
